# Sharing the app

Live: https://zbrojnikviz.dravec.org · Repo: https://github.com/Chartres/zbrojni-kviz

Be a good citizen: post where project shares / "how do I prep" questions belong, disclose
it's free, open-source, made-for-myself and **unofficial**. Don't spam multiple groups at once.

## Where the audience is (Czech firearms / licence community)

- **Reddit – r/czech** — large general CZ sub; firearms/zbrojní-oprávnění threads do well as a
  "made this, sharing it free" post. (Also **r/europeanguns** — EU gun owners, English ok.)
- **LEX z.s. (lex.cz)** — the main CZ gun-owners' rights association; active **Facebook** presence and
  community — the most on-target audience for prep tools.
- **gunlex.cz** — major Czech firearms news portal with an engaged readership/comments.
- **zbrane.cz** — community + a popular "testové otázky" prep section; people there are literally
  studying for the exam.
- **Facebook groups** — search "Zbrojní průkaz / Zbrojní oprávnění", "Sportovní střelba", "Začínáme
  se zbraní", and local střelnice/club groups. These prep-focused groups convert best.
- **Hunters** — myslivost.cz forums / ČMMJ club channels (myslivci need the oprávnění too).
- **forum.valka.cz** — long-running CZ weapons forum (more enthusiast/history than prep).
- **Discord** — a few CZ sport-shooting servers; share in an off-topic/tools channel.
